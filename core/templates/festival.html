One Scriba Festival

Every year we celebrate writing in all its forms—fiction, screenwriting, and theatre. Three categories, one mission: shining a light on the authors behind each story.

One Scriba curates a series of creative writing festivals each year. Every edition focuses on a specific craft—fiction, screenwriting, or theatre—offering cash prizes and visibility to outstanding authors.

The festival blends readings, workshops, and roundtables with industry professionals, creating a meeting point for writers, editors, and readers.

Categories

  • Short fiction and novels
  • Screenwriting
  • Playwriting and dramaturgy
Stories fill the world; the festival is where they come together.

Prizes and recognition

Each category awards cash prizes to the top entries. Winners and finalists are featured in the Authors section and highlighted in One Scriba Magazine. Every shortlisted work receives an official mention.

Grand prize
Recognition for the overall winner of each category.
Cash awards
Dedicated prizes for the top-ranked entries in every category.
Honourable mentions
All finalists receive an official mention and festival badge.
